Short, academically framed modules for NHS clinical learners and university students. Covers cataract and glaucoma with OSCE-style checklists and NICE / NICE CKS aligned safety points.

What’s included

  • Module 1: Cataract (assessment + referral principles)
  • Module 2: Glaucoma (open-angle overview + acute angle-closure red flags)
  • OSCE-style checklist (eye exam structure; teaching-adapted)
  • Short formative quizzes (self-check)

Intended for education/learning. Always follow local trust pathways and supervision requirements.

How students can use it

  • Before clinic: skim the module summaries
  • Before OSCE: print checklist as a one-page PDF
  • After learning: do the quiz and repeat missed items

Evidence base

Content is framed to align with NICE / NICE CKS safety principles and referral guidance, with OSCE structure referencing Geeky Medics eye examination resources (adapted for teaching).
